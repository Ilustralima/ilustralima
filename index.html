Reaphook Movie Poster

Poster Design

Poster design for the academic live action short 'Reaphook'. A fantasy film about a new origin story of Captain Hook, targeting young adults.

The illustration was based on the three main actors' reference, digital painting in photoshop. In this new version created by Kelsey Pigden, the antagonism between Captain Hook and Peter Pan is based on a revenge, the roles are inverted and Pan assumes the archetype of villain.

In the final poster, 24" x 36", the illustration was resized giving to the logo more air to breath and more empty space in the final composition.

Redo Clocks

Game Project

Redo Clocks is a platform game with steampunk and fantasy thematics for windows. Join Redo in her/his journey to save the people that have been transformed in robots, in the Land of the Clocks.

Some of the character design process below

The project has started in a course on my design undergraduate program and it has been made in Game Maker engine. I work mainly on the concept art, assets, interface and game design. The team is completed with Anderson Henrique, who takes care of the game mechanics, interface and game design and Mary Kelly, writer, game mechanics and player`s feedback.

Tribal Dance

Game Project

Tribal Dance was a project created for web browsers in 48 hours in the last Global Game Jam, hosted by Igda Recife.

A game about a broke guy called Cid that realizes an opportunity to make some bucks pretending he is an aborigine. He creates a tribal dance show called "Ritual", with cheap stuff bought in garage shops. The game itself is a point-and-click, whose player needs to pay attention to the required kind of dance asked by the audience by clicking in the respective orbs, which set the dance that is performed by Cid.

Above some of the character design process

My work was concentrated on the art and game design side, with the contribution of Anderson Henrique and Camila Lima on GD. Danilo Borges made our switch to Construct 2 engine, Eduardo D'Antona AKA Dude contributed with his great audio design. We also had the collaboration of Luana Nascimento and Felipe Pereira. Because all of these great guys we could deliver a playable version of the game available online. here.
